Overview

The Concept

Researching and planning the addition of a rolling library ladder to our bespoke built-in bookshelf unit in the living room. The project aims to combine visual flair with practical access to the top shelves.

Ceiling Height
2.7m
Est. Ladder length
2.4m
Hardware Type
Rolling
Design Details

Key Considerations

  • Rail Mounting: To be mounted at the very top of the unit, spanning full width.
  • Clearance: Top section is recessed — rail position needs careful measuring to clear lower shelf fronts as the ladder swings.
  • Home Position: Left or right column to keep the TV area clear when not in use.
  • Recommended Finish: Dark matte black or dark bronze hardware — complements the sage green paint beautifully.
